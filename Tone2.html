Tawhid Ar Ruboobiyyah

Tawheed Ar Ruboobiyah is the category of monotheism which focuses on the Lordship of Allah. Belief in Tawheed Ar Ruboobiyyah is the belief of Allah being the sole Lord in creating and sustaining everything. Even the Kuffar have this Tahwheed in their belief.
